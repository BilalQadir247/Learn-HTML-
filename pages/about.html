Our Dream

Our Story

Beginning: March 10, 2026

The world of programming often intimidates beginners. When we saw that people wanted to learn coding but couldn't find a safe and easy way, we founded Learn HTML. Our journey began on March 10, 2026, and today we provide free education to thousands of students.

What We Provide ?

We have built this website like a complete academy:

  • 80+ Structured Tutorials: From basic HTML to advanced HTML5 features, everything in sequence.

  • Real-Time Code Editor: No software needed to learn coding. Just write with our built-in editor and see instant results.

  • 10+ Hands-on Practice Tasks: Tasks that will open your logical mind.

  • 70+ Interactive Quiz Questions: A professional JavaScript-based test that will boost your confidence.
